What is FlowCerts?

FlowCerts

FlowCerts is an educational certificate issuing and verification platform built on the Flow blockchain and Chainlink technologies.
We use these technologies to ensure the security, verifiability and transparency of educational certificates issued on FlowCerts.

How does it work?

Using FlowCerts

Certificates issued using FlowCerts are backed by Flow's robust blockchain network and securely stored on many machines worldwide.

Issue Certificates

To issue certificates on FlowCerts, your institution may sign up and verify its identity on our website. Once verified, it may begin issuing certificates to students.

Institution Signup / Login

Share Certificates

Once your institution / school issues certificates to students using FlowCerts, unique links are generated which allow students to share their certificates with others, such as potential employers.

Verify Certificates

Using just a given FlowCerts PDF or link, employers, institutions or students alike can instantly verify a FlowCerts certificate's authenticity using our Verification Tool, making it difficult to forge a FlowCerts certificate.

How does FlowCerts secure and verify certificates?

FlowCerts stores your certificates on the decentralised and battle-tested Flow blockchain either in encrypted or unencrypted form.

We then generate a unique link where the certificate may be verified, which students may share with employers or put on their resume.

FlowCerts can also embed a verification card containing a QR code to verify the certificate and details such as institution name, etc.

When anyone uses the unique link to verify the certificate, FlowCerts checks the contents of the certificate and institution against the entries stored on the Flow blockchain, protecting against tampering, forgery and impersonation.
